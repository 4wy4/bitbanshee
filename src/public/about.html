BitBanshee: Get Your Updates. Up to Speed.

BitBanshee is a new tool that uses binary differentiation to shrink update size.
The algorithm behind BitBanshee uses a combination of compression algorithms and deep pattern comparisons to isolate only the minimum number of bits in the binary exectuable that need to be changed in order to transform it from one executable to another.
By only downloading the bits your computer needs, your consumers' update size-and your bandwidth use-can decrease from a gigabyte to 3 megabytes - instantly.
