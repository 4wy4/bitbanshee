BitFlogger: Updates on Your Time

BitFlogger is a new tool that uses binary differentiation to shrink update size.
By only downloading the bits your program needs, your consumers' update size-and your bandwidth use-
can decrease from a gigabyte to 3 megabytes-instantly.

Fast

Updates arrive and install in the blink of an eye, ensuring that your users won't have to wait at all for a new change to come through.

Light

Smaller patch files mean faster updates, which means less bandwidth and less unnecessary traffic.

Secure

Since you're shipping small diff files instead of huge binary blobs, the risk of any cohesive data leak is much lower. And they're encrypted with the same keys as your normal updates.
